SHORTCUT INVADERS

Alien ships are Excel shortcuts.
Type the correct key combo to destroy them!

HOW TO PLAY

Enemies show their shortcut name.
Press the matching keys to blast them.
Wrong combo = miss (-10pts). Let them land = lose a life.

Ctrl, Shift, Alt + key combinations supported
